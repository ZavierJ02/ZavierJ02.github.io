Printing & Mailing FAQ

Printing FAQ

Q: Can you print in color?
A: Yes, we can print in both color and black and white.
Q: How long does it take to get something printed?
A: Many simple projects can be done in a day or so, but plan for a few days. Contact campusservices@grandview.edu or visit Campus Services to confirm turnaround times.
Q: Can you print window decals?
A: No, we currently do not offer window decal printing.
Q: What sizes can you print?
A: From business cards up to 42 inches wide by 10 feet long—we can print a wide variety of sizes.
Q: Can I use my Papercut account to pay?
A: No. Printing projects through Campus Services must be paid with cash or card.
Q: How can I submit a printing job?
A:
  • For departments/clubs: submit via GV Print Shop site.
  • For personal/class projects: email files and instructions to campusservices@grandview.edu.
Q: How can I order Grand View Business Cards?
A: Use this form: Business Card Orders.
Q: What file types can I submit?
A: PDF is preferred, but we also accept Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator), and common image formats (JPG, PNG, TIFF).
Q: Can I submit personal print projects?
A: Yes! Just email your project and details. University-related projects take precedence.

Mailing FAQ

Q: What is the price of a first-class stamp?
A: As of 00/00/2025, first-class stamps are $0.73. Check the USPS site for updates.
Q: Can I mail/ship things from GV?
A: Yes! Bring your item to Campus Services (Mon–Fri, 8am–4pm) for USPS, FedEx, or UPS shipping.
Q: How much will it cost to send my package?
A: Costs vary by weight, size, and speed. Ground shipping is typically $10–$30.*
*Based on historical pricing.
Q: Do I need a box to ship my package?
A: Boxing and sealing before arrival is best. Supplies are available at the GV Bookstore.
Q: How long does it take mail to arrive?
A: First-class mail: 3–10 days. Ground shipping: 2–7 days. Express is available.
Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: No. Use USPS, FedEx, or UPS for international shipments. Nearest USPS: 1501 E Fleming Ave.
Q: If I have a letter coming, can I pick it up in Campus Services?
A: No, letters are placed in student mailboxes by 3pm daily.
Q: Will I get notified if I get a package?
A: Yes. Wait for an email from Campus Services before picking up your package.
